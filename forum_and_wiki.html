The Workshop Guide

A quick walkthrough of how we collaborate.

The Digital Living Room

The Forum is where we hang out. It's for the high-frequency "chatter" of the workshop. Use this for introductions, troubleshooting, and daily logs.

Finding your Team

Want to start a project but need a 3D Modeler or a Writer? This is the place to pitch ideas and find your crew in the "Looking for Group" section.

The Library of Truth

The Wiki is for things that shouldn't disappear. It is a structured home for project manifests, asset lists, and hardware specs.

The Blueprint Gallery

If you've mastered a Libre tool, share the knowledge. Create "How-To" pages that remain editable by the community.

The Cycle of Creation

Discussion happens in the forum; documentation lives in the wiki. This prevents great knowledge from being lost in a scrolling chat history.

Start a thread → Get feedback → Move final docs to the Wiki.

Community Guidelines

Focus on Libre Software

We prioritize tools that respect user freedom. We advocate for open alternatives whenever possible.

The "No-Expert" Rule

Treat everyone as a peer. There are no "dumb questions" here. We value curiosity.

Celebrate the Unfinished

Don't be afraid to post a half-baked idea. Our community exists to help you bridge the gap.
